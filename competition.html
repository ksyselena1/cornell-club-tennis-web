Competition

Every year, Cornell Club Tennis travels around the nation to compete in tournaments against other college club teams. Our club team has historically been one of the strongest in the nation, placing 4th in the nation in 2016 and 11th in the nation in 2017. We participate in a variety of national, sectional, and invitational tournaments, including:

The Battle of the Sections is a tournament held in Bethany Beach, DE. This tournament has teams from all across the east coast, giving us an opportunity to compete against teams from the Mid-Atlantic and our own Eastern section.
